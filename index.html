The premise

It is 1428. In the history he remembers, the Roman Empire has twenty-five years left — the Morea overrun, the City in flames, and Constantine Palaiologos dead and unrecognised on its walls. A modern mind now wears that prince's face, and it intends to spend those twenty-five years very differently.
